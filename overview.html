Overview

The main objective of this website is to provide relief to affected low class, middle class families and migrant workers during the covid crisis.

The fund collected will help affected families to buy groceries and basic needs

We can utilize the youth force to help the needy through volunteership

The main vision of this initiation is to make our people and our country abundantly happy and rich - We are Rebels with a Cause

How It Works??

step 1: Visit the Ask for help form.

step 2: Fill the forms to the best of your knowledge.

step 3: Our volunteer team will contact you, understand your needs and complete the verification process.

step 4: Buy the needs which are required for you and our volunteers will transfer the amount to the store directly.
